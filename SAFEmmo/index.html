Community-built MMORPG

History

First mentioned by me--community forum member WhiteOutMashups--a MMORPG built on the SAFE Network allows anybody with an internet connection to submit worlds, characters, weapons, and anything into an ever-evolving game....and get PAID for their creations!

Features

Free to play by default (as with everything on the SAFE Network), a SAFE MMORPG would be an open-source, crowdsourced masterpiece, always evolving with new content, making it a never-ending adventure. Among the most exciting aspects are the abilities to earn REAL MONEY in-game, as the game's economy could easily be based off of digital currencies, including SAFE's own SafeCoin. In addition to being able to earn your entire income by playing such a game, you could also submit new content and characters, which you could sell to the game to be voted on & added into the next update, for everyone to enjoy.

Launch date

It was expected that the network would launch at the end of last year, (and this app soon after,) however an amazing new coding language came out called "Rust" that turned out to be perfect for the SAFE Network to be created with. So, the team decided to restart the whole network from scratch in Rust. This means we will have to wait a few more months to see the network or this app. Follow it on Github!
